Movies Share
Li'l Home Theatre
The app will facilitate the users to watch movies in sync with each other on their individual devices and allows the near by users to watch movies in sync.
Project Description
There are the situations when we have to travel for long hours and have nothing to do. Consider the same situation along with your family or friends. You can make your journey the best moments of the life. The App allows the users to pair their near by devices with each other and watch the movies in sync in all the devices without using any internet. The app comes with various features like text in common chat, send voice messages and many more.
